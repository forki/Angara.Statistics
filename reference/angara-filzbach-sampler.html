continuemcmc(...)
Signature: (sampler:Sampler * logl:(Parameters -> float) * burnCount:int * sampleCount:int * thinning:int option) -> SamplerResult

Continuation of sampling procedure after incomplete burn-in. It does burnCount additional burn-in iterations followed by collecting sampleCount samples from posterior. Total number of iterations is burnCount + thinning * sampleCount.
